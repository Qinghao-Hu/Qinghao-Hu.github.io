Qinghao Hu (胡擎昊)

I am a Research Assistant Professor at S-Lab, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from NTU in 2023, advised by Prof. Tianwei Zhang and Prof. Yonggang Wen. Before that, I received my M.Sc. degree from National University of Singapore in 2020 and my B.Eng. degree from Zhejiang University in 2018. I am fortunate to be awarded the Google PhD Fellowship.
